# Fabricks DWH — Acronyms & Abbreviations

| Acronym | Meaning | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **SPT** | Sanitary Business Unit | Sanitary product division |
| **GBM** | Building Material Business Unit | Building materials product division |
| **BU** | Business Unit | Org hierarchy L0 (country) through L8 (location) |
| **SCM** | Supply Chain Management | Business unit structure for procurement |
| **MDM** | Master Data Management | Article deduplication and data quality system |
| **SCD1** | Slowly Changing Dimension Type 1 | Overwrites history; no tracking needed |
| **SCD2** | Slowly Changing Dimension Type 2 | Preserves history with `__valid_from` / `__valid_to` |
| **UDF** | User Defined Function | Custom SQL functions in `fabricks/udfs/` |
| **IVP** | Incentive/Margin Bonus Program | Adjusted margin including supplier and article bonuses |
| **HEP** | High Engagement Program | Product category with dedicated budget and margin targets |
| **OTA** | One Time Article | Template/single-use articles; excluded from regular sales analysis |
| **EAN** | European Article Number | Barcode identifier (stored as `bar_code`) |
| **COMEX** | Commercial Excellence | Campaign and promotion management system |
| **BME** | Parent Company Group | Used for product taxonomy classification |
| **CREFO** | Creditreform | External credit rating / supplier verification service |
| **CIP** | Customer Interaction Portal | External product catalog distribution |
| **GL** | General Ledger | Accounting entries and financial postings |
| **NPS** | Net Promoter Score | Customer satisfaction survey data |
| **DMS** | Document Management System | |
